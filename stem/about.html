About Us

At ATC, we have a group of committed and competent Professionals with proven technical know -how in STEM who have had the best of education and trainings combine with multiple years of experience with many success stories both in Nigeria and abroad.

Our Stem Laboratory is well equipped with modern facilities that may not be ordinarily available elsewhere due to cost and logistics of procurement. However, the desire to offer the best to our clients has propelled us into acquiring them with utmost delight. Of course, this does not only make the job of transferring the knowledge much more easier for us but it actually help in attracting the interest of young children to wanting to learn.
At ATC, we are giving more at a relatively low cost comparatively. We make bold to say that the price of our product and services are the most financially friendly anyone can find around when put side by side with the quality of service we render. Our interest is to rouse the curiosity of young children and to make them top STEM technocrats in the future above other considerations.
We have the capacity to provide and meet up with personal expectations of our esteemed clients. Yours is to say it and we make it happen.

Africa trainovation consulting limited (ATC) is a digital hub for innovative and transformational trainings, skills acquisition and consultancy for working professionals, organizations, companies and the society at large. ATC is situated in Abeokuta, Ogun state, Nigeria and founded in 2019.
